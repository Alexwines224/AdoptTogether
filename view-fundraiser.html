Carter De Angelis's 24th Birthday by Carter De Angelis

Hey Everyone, I'm turning 24 on April 25th. That's way too old for presents but not too old to raise money for an awesome cause. Help me raise some money for this awesome family so they can adopt a precious little girl from the US. Adoptions are expensive, We can help! If all of my facebook friends give $10, they'll have enough to adopt 3 little girls. Please consider giving, you are a huge blessing.
We are raising $40,000 to cover the cost of this adoption and anything above and beyond what we need will be used for the following: 50% will go for a future adoption into our family and 50% will go to a family in need of extra funds to bring their baby home.
Please understand how crucial you are in our ability to adopt. We truly feel we are to do this without going into debt and yet have 100% peace about the Lord's ability to supernaturally provide for our sweet prince or princess. Once our home study is complete, we need to raise $20,000 before our profile can be given to birth moms. Reason being, once we are selected, the funds must be immediately transferred. Thank you for partnering with us to make a dream come true and add another bundle of joy, life and love into our family! We love you.
