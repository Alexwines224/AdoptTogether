FAQ

Are my donations tax deductible?
Yes! Your donation is going to the Hoping Hearts foundation and then it is being appropriated to the families.
Can I send a check?
Of course! Just make your check payable to AdoptTogether and put the family’s name on the envelope & in the memo and mail it to:
AdoptTogether
251 W. Central Ave. #278
Springboro, OH 45066
1 (888) 826-0948
Can I give a monthly contribution?
Yes, on the last step we’ll ask if you want to make this a monthly contribution.
Can I edit or cancel my monthly contribution?
You can view or edit your monthly contribution at any time here.
