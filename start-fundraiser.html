FAQ

What is the approval process for families listed on AdoptTogether.org?
The most common "hang ups" families experience are completing their home study or receiving a letter of approval from their agency.
How long does it take for my profile to go live?
Once you have submitted your profile request, you will receive the completion form within one business day. After that, timing will depend on how quickly you fill out and return the remaining forms. Some profiles have gone live within 24 hours and some have taken a month.
